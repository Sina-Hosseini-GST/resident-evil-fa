REDOCS SELECT A GAME

Hi, I'm Sina, and this is my independent project, to translate the whole in-game documents of each Resident Evil into Persian! Go select your desired title and explore the story!

This website is constantly being updated, and this means that every time you enter REdocs, you're likely to see more CONTENT! So, stay tuned!
